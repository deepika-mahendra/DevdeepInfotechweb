About Our Company

Founded in year 2022, Devdeep is a committed and determined quality software exporter, Raipur Chhattisgarh based private company(Company limited by shares). Our aim is to develop and promote advanced information technologies in order to address the present-day challenges. Our general purpose is to fulfill the gap between the service providers and the organizations who are willing to adopt that technology. Our mission is to assure the highest quality products and total client satisfaction with reduced delivery time through a friendly company staff. This should provide a holistic approach to hospital industries and Residential Complex.

Kuldip Mahendra

Founder and Director

Devdeep System Pvt. Ltd.

In this era of digitalization, this pandemic has taught us that whatever we were doing from our offices, we could do the same with the comfort and safety of our respective homes. This was only possible because of the innovation in Information Technology. In the 18 years of my career, I have observed and understood the IT sector closely, from hardware to software, from network to communication and interface. I would like to fill the gap between service providers who are developing the idea and organizations who would like to adopt that technology. I will feel blessed to deliver the technology to those who actually need it.
